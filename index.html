Planners = Bad

Take a look at yours. Planners are a pain to use. They were designed for having a job. You don't. You're a student. You need a better planner. You need PlanHW.

Unclutter your life with PlanHW.

Smart Reminders

Setting a reminder for when it's due doesn't help you at all. It just sounds an alarm on your phone... in class. How in the world is that going to help you? It isn't. PlanHW learns when you do homework, and alerts you when you can do it. Not when it's due, just when you can do it. And you don't have to do anything.

Quick Type

Instead of filling out fields on when to do your homework, type what it is. PlanHW is smart enough to know that "LA paper due Monday" means that you have a paper due Monday for LA. This gives you enough time to actually pass in your passing period.

Gamification

Doing homework is boring. Luckily, we teamed up with Egan White to make doing homework fun again. With an easy to use gamification API, developers from around the world will be able to make interactive RPG adventures that you complete by doing homework.

Who are we?

Ben

Ben, a student programmer interested in all sorts of technology, thinks planners are awful. So, he took it upon himself to fix that. As the CEO and lead developer of PlanHW, he hopes to change how you do your homework.

Egan

Egan White, a "highly trained unprofessional" student, loves the idea of turning your homework into a game. He's working on the API for developers to make RPG games that you will be able to complete by doing homework.
